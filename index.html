About Coca-Cola

Coca-Cola is one of the most iconic brands in the world, with a history over 135 years old. Since its creation in 1886 by Dr. John Pemberton, Coca-Cola has become a global symbol of refreshment, joy, and togetherness.

Today, Coca-Cola operates in more than 200 countries and offers a wide range of beverages including Coca-Cola Classic, Diet Coke, Coca-Cola Zero Sugar, and many more. We focus on community, sustainability, and happiness around the world.

  • 🌍 Available in 200+ countries worldwide
  • 🥤 Over 1.9 billion servings consumed every day
  • 💡 Focus on sustainability and eco-friendly packaging
  • 🎉 Bringing people together through events and campaigns

Our mission: refresh the world and make a difference. Coca-Cola isn’t just a drink—it’s a lifestyle!
